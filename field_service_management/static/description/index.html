Field Service drives profitability by automating the process of dispatching field technicians to service calls.

  • Customer
    • Customer can raise Job requests and track its status.
    • Customer can check his invoices for the completed jobs.
  • Operator
    • Operator can manage all the jobs in the system.
    • He can configure the service based on his locality and services.
    • He can create invoices for the completed Jobs.
    • He can view the data via insights.
  • Serviceman
    • Serviceman can set his per hour rate and express rate for various services.
    • He can see the jobs assigned to him and manage its lifecycle.
    • He can view his invoices and check his earnings.

Customer Invoices

Express Service

The customer can request for express service in case of Urgency. The express charges can be decided by the Serviceman. The express charges will be added to the invoice automatically.

Localization Configuration

Configure the State, City, Area the Serviceman works. The jobs will be allocated to the Serviceman using this data.
