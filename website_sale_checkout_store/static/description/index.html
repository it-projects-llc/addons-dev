Pickup and pay at store

Simplify checkout process by excluding shipping and\or payment information

On checkout step customer selects the shipping and billing variant. If chosen variant requires not "payment" payment step will be skipped. Below introduced all variants and according to it fillable fields.

Pickup and pay at store

Pickup at store but pay now

Pay now and get delivery

Pay on delivery

Administrator may configure which of these option need to be used.
