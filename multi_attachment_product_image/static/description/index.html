Upload Multiple Images at Once

The great tool to upload multiple images to product at once

By default, you are able to upload only a single image to product at once in Odoo. The module facilitates this process by uploading multiple images at once.

How It Works

  • Open product form
  • Go to Images tab
  • Click Select Images


Select several images at once and click Ok


As a result, all the selected images are uploaded
